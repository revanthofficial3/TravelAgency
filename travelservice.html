America Tourist Visa Required Documents

  1. The U.S. Consulate General, India processes applications for both nonimmigrant and immigrant visas to the United States.
  2. Nonimmigrant visas are for people visiting the U.S. temporarily for tourism, business, education, medical treatment, or petition-based employment. The type of visa required depends on your purpose of travel.
  3. Immigrant visas are for applicants planning to permanently relocate to the United States. Consular Districts The U.S. Embassy, New Delhi provides consular services in the states of Delhi, Jammu and Kashmir, Punjab, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh and Uttarakhand, and the country of Bhutan. If you are not in the Delhi Consular district, find your nearest consulate below.

Chennai

The Consulate General in Chennai provides consular services for the states of Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Kerala, and the Union Territories of Lakshadweep islands, Pondicherry, and the Union Territories of Andaman & Nicobar Islands.

Hyderabad

The Consulate General in Hyderabad provides consular services for the state of Andhra Pradesh.

kolkata

The Consulate General in Kolkata provides consular services for the states of West Bengal, Bihar, Jharkhand, Nagaland, Mizoram, Manipur, Meghalaya, Orissa, Arunachal Pradesh, Sikkim, and Assam.

Mumbai

The Consulate General in Mumbai provides consular services for the states of Maharashtra, Goa, Chhattisgarh, Madhya Pradesh, Gujarat, and the Union Territories of Daman and Diu, and Dadra and Nagar Haveli. Philiphines Tourist Visa Required Documents

  1. Original Valid passport with at least six month validity from the date of travel (Old Passports Mandatory).
  2. 1 visa application Form (Form Should be checked for Single Entry Visa only as the Embassy does not issues Multiple Entry Visas) .
  3. 1 Photograph (Fresh ones i.e. NOT used earlier for any visa). (with white background with 35 x 45mm Without Cross sign).
  4. Photocopy of the passport (1st page and last page) is MUST
  5. Covering letter addressed to The Visa Officer, Embassy of Phillipines on Letterhead.
  6. E ticket with ticket number showing departure after 10 working days from the date of submission
  7. Stay Itinerary (hotel)
  8. Applicant`s direct contact number, either landline OR mobile
  9. Personal Bank Statement for last Six months in original with original bank seal with minimum 1,25,000 balance .
  10. Last six months payslip
  11. NOC/Leave approval letter from employer
  12. Last 3 years ITR OF applicant with BARCODE
  13. In case of a married couple travelling together Marriage Certificate is required to be attested and embassy can also ask the same for any couple at random.
  14. If Children below 15 years not accompanied by the Parents then VEG is required to be attested.[Note: Veg is a noc Letter signed by both the parents on a Stamp Paper attested by Local Govt.
  15. If Spouse is Philippino then Spouse Name should be indicated in the Visa Applicants passport copy and report of Marriage issued by the Philippines Embassy or Philippines Govt.

Processing Time:10 Working DaysAfter submission

SCHENGEN Tourist Visa Required Documents

  1. Existing Schengen countries are as follows:
  2. Austria,Germany,Netherlands,Belgium,Greece,Norway,Denmark,
    Iceland,Portugal,Finland,Italy,Spain,France,Luxembourg,Sweden.
  3. The following Countries joined the Schengen Agreement on 21st December 2007
  4. Czech Republic,Hungary,Estonia,Latvia,Lithuania,Malta,Poland,
    Slovakia,Slovenia
  5. Travelling in Europe has been simplified with the introduction of the Schengen visa. As a visitor to the Schengen area, you will enjoy the many advantages of this unified visa system.
  6. Generally speaking with a Schengen visa, you may enter one country and travel freely throughout the Schengen region during the validity of the visa. Internal border controls are limited with no or few stops and checks.

Processing Time: 6 Working Days After submission
